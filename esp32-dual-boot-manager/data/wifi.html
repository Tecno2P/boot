    Mode Control

    AP Mode: Device broadcasts its own hotspot (SSID: ESP32-DualBoot).
    STA Mode: Device connects to your home/office network.

    Switching mode clears saved credentials and reboots the device.
    Switch to AP Mode?
    This will clear saved credentials and reboot the device into AP mode. Connect to ESP32-DualBoot (password: admin1234) at 192.168.4.1.
    Clear Saved Credentials?
    This will delete the saved SSID and password from NVS flash. The device will use AP mode on the next reboot.
